Lionel Messi

The Legendary Football Player

Here's a timeline of Lionel Messi's life and career:

  • 1987 - Born in Rosario, Argentina
  • 1995 - Joins the youth academy of Newell's Old Boys in Rosario
  • 2000 - Joins the youth academy of Barcelona FC in Spain
  • 2004 - Makes his debut for Barcelona's first team
  • 2005 - Becomes the youngest player to score a goal for Barcelona
  • 2006 - Wins his first La Liga title with Barcelona
  • 2008 - Wins his first Champions League title with Barcelona
  • 2009 - Wins his first Ballon d'Or award as the best player in the world
  • 2010 - Leads Argentina to the 2010 FIFA World Cup quarter-finals
  • 2011 - Wins his second Ballon d'Or award
  • 2012 - Scores a record 91 goals in a calendar year
